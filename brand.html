Brand Story Design System

Our Story

Charitable Impact is for anyone who wants to make a difference. Our Impact Account is like an online bank account for charitable giving, and it helps you create the change you want to see in the world.

The brighter future you imagine is worth having

We’re here to help you make it happen. Everyone has the right to resources that help them create change in the world—no matter what causes they choose to support, how much they give, or how experienced they are with charitable giving.

Who we’re for

We’re for anyone who wants to make a difference. In other words, we’re for donors—someone who wants to help others and the world we share, and who gives something towards creating change. No matter what you give, every contribution you make towards creating positive change in the world counts.

What problem do we solve?

Wanting to help others comes naturally. Knowing how to make a difference through charitable giving doesn’t.

We believe in your power to
create change.

Everyone has something they want to change in the world.

Everyone has something to give.

When we give, we get something in return.

Our name

Charitable Impact
Where the desire to help others meets action and impact.
Charitable: adjective, full of love for and goodwill toward others.
Impact: noun, the effect or influence of one person, thing, or action, on another.
