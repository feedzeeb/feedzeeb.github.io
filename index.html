Why Proxima Nova

It's geometric, sleek, modern and flexible.

Proxima Nova is a geometric typeface that emphasizes simplicity and clarity. It’s recognizable and easy to read, and was chosen to signal a modern and optimistic approach to charitable giving.

Because the brighter future you imagine is worth having

We know that giving can and should be an enjoyable, uplifting, and personally meaningful experience. We see a future where this is the norm, not the exception.

Charitable Impact operates as a donor-advised fund. In short, this means you can manage all of your charitable giving from a single account, which we call the Impact Account. When you add money to your Impact Account, you're actually making a donation to Charitable Impact Foundation, a registered charity and public foundation. It's why you get a tax receipt after adding money. The funds you add stay in your account until you decide you'd like to send gifts to charities, Giving Groups, and other people on Charitable Impact.

Colour

Our 16 colours share the warmth of our voice while the breadth of colour references the personal nature of giving. The large palette allows the brand to remain dynamic and malleable without being subject to strict rules and limitations. Liquid branding makes it easier to adapt the brand to different types of content, while keeping it fresh and relevant to any given context.

Consistency

With a liquid branding approach, elements of the brand share a common backbone while remaining responsive to different contexts and ever-changing needs. The brand continues to feel fresh yet familiar. It can easily scale to fit future applications without the need for a complete redesign.

Motion

Where possible, brand elements demonstrate or imply continuous forward motion. This emphasis on movement echoes the desire for progression towards a better world and the need to take action.

Our system of illustration is also a visual language.

It helps us explain abstract concepts and demonstrate the different motivations of donors creating change in the world. The donor often remains front and centre so that the audience can more easily see themselves in each scene. Petra Sitaru, a renowned illustrator and designer, was selected to create Charitable Impact's illustration language.

Expressed through a range of people and environments.

Small, joyful moments and vibrant surroundings signal the brighter future that awaits. At Charitable Impact, we're for everyone. Our approach to illustration aims to reflect this position, embracing diversity and inclusivity.
